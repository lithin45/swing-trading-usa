# Validation artifacts — how this strategy earns (or loses) trust

This directory is the **persistent, versioned record** the mandate (§7) and the
2026-06-10 audit (P1 #3/#5) require. Nothing in here lives only in commit
messages or session notes anymore.

## Contents

- **`trials.jsonl`** — the trial ledger: one row for every configuration
  evaluation a human looked at, append-only, duplicate-id-guarded.
  `purpose=selection` rows are what the Deflated Sharpe's N counts;
  `validation` rows are out-of-sample confirmations; `robustness` rows are
  sweep cells (looked at, never used to re-tune).
- **`sweep-<start>-<end>.md`** — ±10% parameter-sensitivity sweeps
  (generated by `scripts/sweep_sensitivity.py`): the plateau check + PSR/DSR +
  CSCV PBO for the deployed config on that window.
- **`curves/<window>/<variant>.csv`** — per-period return curves persisted by
  the validation/sweep scripts, so CSCV PBO is recomputable without re-running
  backtests (the 2026-06-11 session lost its curves to `/tmp`; never again).

## Paper-history void + what live actually runs (2026-06-12)

All live paper history recorded **before 2026-06-12** is VOID as go/no-go
evidence: the live account was drifting from the validated config on three axes
*silently* (chandelier trail in legacy mode, 0.20-weight news factor, universe
superset). **The 3–6 month clock of go/no-go #6 restarts at the next deploy.**

**Owner decision (2026-06-12, recorded):** live deliberately runs a VARIANT of
the validated combo, now as explicit config rather than drift:

- `factors.news_sentiment` weight **0.20 live** (owner choice). The factor is
  structurally inert in every backtest (no point-in-time news on free data), so
  live paper trading is its only test bed; scores persist to the DB for
  after-the-fact attribution.
- `universe.sp500_only: false` (owner choice): thematic + news-discovered names
  are tradable and consume budget slots. Every validated holdout traded
  point-in-time S&P 500 only.
- `exits.trail_legacy_stop`: **OFF — decided by the r5 experiment, 2026-06-12**
  (base vs legacy_trail, three dev windows, all six rows ledgered). The trail
  lost decisively on 2015-16 (−0.134R vs −0.009R) and 2022-24 (−0.048R vs
  +0.067R, flipping the best window negative) and won only narrowly in the
  pure 2020-21 trend (+0.015R vs +0.004R, maxDD −12.7% vs −14.7%); summed,
  ~+10R (fixed stop) vs ~−25R (trail) on the same signals. The owner reviewed
  the numbers and kept the fixed stop. The flag remains wired into live and
  the backtest legacy path identically, so the variant stays one line away
  and re-testable.

Consequences, so nobody mistakes the record later: paper results measure the
LIVE VARIANT, not the validated combo, and may not be quoted against the
holdout numbers without saying so. The shadow outcome tracker deliberately
stays on the validated no-trail reference model, so the reconciliation report's
live-vs-shadow R delta is a running measurement of what the variant's exits
contribute; news/theme attribution comes from the persisted scores and
universe-source tags.

## The go/no-go checklist (real capital)

All of the following, simultaneously, before any non-paper deployment:

1. **Expectancy > 0 and PF ≥ 1.2 on ≥ 2 untouched OOS windows.** (The
   pre-2026-06-11 "+0.11R / PF 1.27" numbers were contaminated — cache holes,
   zombie signals, no halt replay; see `docs/strategy-uplift-2026-06-11.md`.
   The honest holdout status lives in that report's §3.)
2. **DSR ≥ 0.95 vs the ledger's selection count** — the edge must survive its
   own selection process (see the latest sweep file for the current number and
   its var(SR)-proxy caveat).
3. **CSCV PBO < 0.5** on the relevant variant family.
4. **±10% sweep verdict: plateau** — no single threshold's ±10% move erases
   the edge.
5. **Cadence proof** — backtest entry histogram respects the ≤7/month ceiling
   with the bar (not the cap) doing most of the work in normal months.
6. **3–6 months of live paper tracking within tolerance of backtest.** This is
   an **execution-fidelity test, not an expectancy test** (reworded 2026-06-12):
   at ≤7 entries/month, 3–6 months yields 21–42 trades, which cannot statistically
   resolve a +0.1–0.19R edge against zero at σ≈1R — that takes hundreds of trades.
   What 21–42 trades CAN prove is whether live execution matches the model the
   backtests assume. Pre-registered tolerance bands (committed 2026-06-12, BEFORE
   the restarted clock accrues data; measured by `tracking/reconcile.py` and the
   dashboard's Execution Quality page):
   - median entry slippage within ±10 bps of the cost model's 10 bps/side;
   - mean |live R − shadow R| on matched closed trades ≤ 0.05R;
   - monthly entry cadence within the backtest's monthly entry-count IQR for the
     matching window;
   - limit fill rate within ±15 pp of the backtest's fill-rate on the 2025-26
     window.
7. **Loss-halt replay ON** in every number used for the decision (live gates
   refuse entries the unhalted backtest would take).

Expected live degradation per the research brief: 20–50% of backtest edge
(McLean-Pontiff). A go decision should still clear zero after that haircut.

### Window-report caveat (2026-06-12)

2025-26 — the lone robust holdout pass on a COMPLETE universe — is
**in-sample-in-spirit**: the strategy form was chosen during 2024-26 by people
who lived that momentum regime, so soft researcher degrees of freedom flatter
it even though the data is clean. Pre-2020 complete-universe windows (paid
data: Norgate/EODHD) and forward evidence are the only fully clean channels;
weigh 2013-14/2017-19 weakness MORE, not less, on this account.

## Roadmap deltas (2026-06-12)

- **Vol-managed exposure (was Tier-1 #3 in `docs/strategy-review-2026-06-10.md`)
  is DEMOTED to "revisit only with live evidence":** its Barroso–Santa-Clara
  premise (binary regime cliff + full nominal sizing) died with the non-absorbing
  brake and the size-strangulation finding (effective risk ~0.3% vs 1% nominal
  from compounding multipliers). Building another sizing dial on a stack that
  already over-penalizes would burn trials on a saturated knob class.

## Stopping rule (committed 2026-06-12)

If, after the clean re-validations (2020-21 re-run, clean sweeps + CSCV PBO)
and at most TWO further pre-registered selection experiments, no configuration
shows **positive alpha over the DD-matched null benchmark on at least two
complete-universe windows**, strategy development freezes and the system runs
ops-only as a learning artifact. (The DD-matched null — same drawdown pain in
an SPY/T-bill blend — is now printed in every window report.)

## Rules of the road

- Every new backtest evaluation that a human looks at gets a ledger row —
  *before* deciding anything based on it.
- Sweeps are robustness checks. Re-tuning a threshold because a sweep cell
  looked better is a NEW selection trial and must be logged as one (and it
  restarts the holdout clock for that parameter).
- Negative results are recorded with the same care as positive ones.
